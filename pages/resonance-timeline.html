Audio Feature
What do these mean?
Energy
Perceived intensity and activity. Loud, fast, noisy tracks (death metal) score high; quiet, slow tracks (a Bach prelude) score low. 0–100%.
Valence
Musical positivity. High valence sounds happy or euphoric; low valence sounds sad, angry, or tense. 0–100%.
Tempo
Estimated speed of the track in beats per minute (BPM). Charted as a 0–200 BPM normalised value.
Danceability
How suitable the track is for dancing — based on rhythm stability, beat strength, and regularity. 0–100%.
